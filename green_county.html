Forest Carbon — Greene County

Aboveground & Belowground Carbon, 1990–2023

About This Tool

This viewer displays annual estimates of forest carbon stored in Greene County tax parcels, derived from Landsat satellite imagery at 30 m resolution. Data spans 1990–2023 and is produced by ESF and Frontier Geospatial.

Two carbon pools are tracked: aboveground carbon (AGC) — carbon stored in live tree biomass above ground — and belowground carbon (BGC) — carbon stored in root systems. Values are expressed as total Mg C stored within each parcel boundary.

Click a parcel on the map to view its carbon history.
